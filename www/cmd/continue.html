continue - Skip to the start of the loop iteration

continue
Used within the body of a while-loop or for-loop, the continue command skips the remainder of the loop body and starts the text iteration.
Examples
for (var i = 10...20) {if (i%2 == 0) continue; if (i%3 == 0) continue; i} 11
